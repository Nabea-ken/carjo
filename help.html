Help Center

Welcome to the Carjo Help Center. Here are answers to the most common questions about buying, renting, and using our platform.

How do I rent a car on Carjo?

Simply browse available vehicles on the Home page, click "Add to Cart" on the one you want, and proceed to checkout. We’ll handle delivery or pickup after confirmation.

What documents are required for car rental?

You need a valid driver’s license, national ID/passport, and in some cases, a refundable deposit.

Do you deliver spare parts and accessories?

Yes, we deliver to most regions. Delivery time and fees depend on your location. Details will be shown at checkout.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is confirmed, tracking info will be available on your Orders page.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, and cash on delivery (for select locations).

I want to sell. How do I register?

Visit our Become a Seller page to learn how to start listing your cars or products.

Need more help?

Contact our support team at support@carjo.com or call +123 456 7890.
