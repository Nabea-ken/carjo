Help Center

Welcome to the Carjo Help Center. Find answers to common questions or reach out for assistance.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I rent a car?
    Visit the home page, browse listings, and click "Add to Cart" then proceed to checkout.
  • How do I become a seller?
    Click the "Become a Seller" link at the top of the page and submit the registration form.
  • Can I track my orders?
    Yes, go to the "Orders" page after logging in to view your rentals and purchases.
  • What payment methods do you accept?
    Currently, we accept credit/debit cards and mobile payments.
